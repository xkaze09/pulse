## HR & People

### Leave Requests & HR Queries
- **Contact:** HR Department
- **Email:** hr@company.com
- **HR Portal:** portal.company.com/leave
- **For:** Annual leave, sick leave, parental leave, compassionate leave, policy questions

### Leave Approval Process
1. Submit request via HR portal
2. Line manager approves/denies within 2 business days
3. HR reviews for leaves > 5 days
4. Confirmation email sent automatically
