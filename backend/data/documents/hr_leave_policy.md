# HR Leave Policy

## Overview

All full-time employees are entitled to paid and unpaid leave as described below. Leave must be requested through the HR portal at least 2 business days in advance (except emergency leave). This policy applies to all permanent employees who have completed their 3-month probationary period.

---

## Annual Leave

- **Entitlement:** 20 working days per calendar year
- **Accrual:** 1.67 days per month of service
- **Carry-over:** Up to 5 unused days may be carried into the next calendar year
- **Minimum request notice:** 2 weeks in advance for requests of 5+ days; 2 business days for shorter periods
- **Approval:** Line manager must approve within 2 business days of submission
- **Payout on exit:** Unused accrued annual leave is paid out upon resignation or termination

---

## Sick Leave

- **Entitlement:** 10 days per calendar year
- **Accrual:** Does not accrue — resets on 1 January each year
- **Uncertified sick leave:** First 3 consecutive days per incident require no medical certificate
- **Certified sick leave:** A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days
- **Long-term illness:** Employees on sick leave for more than 4 weeks are referred to occupational health for assessment
- **Unused sick leave:** Does not carry over and is not paid out on exit

---

## Maternity Leave

- **Duration:** 16 weeks (112 calendar days)
- **Pay:**
  - First 8 weeks: Full basic salary
  - Remaining 8 weeks: 50% of basic salary
- **Notice required:** Minimum 8 weeks written notice before the expected start date
- **Eligibility:** Employees who have been with the company for at least 6 months
- **Return to work:** Employee is guaranteed return to the same or equivalent role

---

## Paternity Leave

- **Duration:** 2 weeks (14 calendar days)
- **Pay:** Full basic salary for the entire 2-week period
- **Timing:** Must be taken within 3 months of the child's birth or adoption date
- **Notice required:** At least 4 weeks in advance where possible
- **Eligibility:** All permanent employees regardless of tenure

---

## Adoption Leave

- **Duration:** Up to 16 weeks, matching maternity leave entitlement
- **Pay:** Same pay structure as maternity leave (first 8 weeks full pay, next 8 weeks 50%)
- **Notice required:** As much notice as reasonably possible

---

## Compassionate Leave

- **Immediate family bereavement (spouse, child, parent, sibling):** Up to 5 paid days
- **Non-immediate family (grandparent, in-law, aunt/uncle):** Up to 3 paid days
- **Additional unpaid:** Up to 5 additional unpaid days available upon request
- **Process:** Notify your manager as soon as possible; HR documents retrospectively

---

## Emergency Leave

- **Entitlement:** Up to 3 days per year for genuine family emergencies (e.g., child illness, sudden dependent care need)
- **Process:** Inform manager on the same day by phone or message; submit HR portal request upon return
- **Pay:** Fully paid; deducted from annual leave if the 3-day limit is exceeded

---

## Unpaid Leave

- **Availability:** After all paid leave entitlements are exhausted
- **Maximum duration:** 4 weeks per calendar year (longer periods require executive approval)
- **Approval:** Requires written approval from both the line manager and HR
- **Benefits:** Statutory benefits continue; company pension contributions are paused for the unpaid period

---

## Study / Exam Leave

- **Entitlement:** Up to 5 days per calendar year for approved professional development exams
- **Eligibility:** Must be enrolled in a company-approved or job-relevant qualification
- **Evidence:** Exam timetable must be submitted to HR at least 4 weeks in advance
- **Pay:** Fully paid

---

## Leave Approval Process

1. Employee logs in to the HR portal and submits a leave request specifying type, dates, and reason.
2. The direct line manager reviews and approves or denies within **2 business days**.
3. For leave requests exceeding **5 working days**, HR Compliance also reviews for entitlement and policy adherence.
4. Employee receives an automated confirmation email once approved.
5. Leave is recorded in the HR system and payroll is updated automatically.
6. Declined requests include a reason; employee may appeal to HR within 5 business days.

---

## Leave Balances

Employees can view their current leave balances at any time via the HR portal under **My Leave → Balance Summary**. Balances shown include:

- Total entitlement for the year
- Days taken year-to-date
- Days remaining
- Pending requests awaiting approval
- Carried-over days (if applicable)
